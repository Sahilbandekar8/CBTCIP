About the Artist

Mahesh Kale is a renowned Indian classical vocalist known for his expertise in khayal and Natya Sangeet. His soulful voice and innovative blend of traditional and contemporary music have earned him acclaim both in India and internationally. Winner of the National Film Award for Best Playback Singer in 2015, Mahesh Kale continues to captivate audiences with his emotive performances and dedication to Hindustani classical music.
